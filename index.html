Open CV Projects

Virtual Painter

this project works on the principle that we can paint anything on a computer screen just by taking that colored pen or anything colored infront of the camera of your system and it works on the application of open CV.

Face Detector

this project works on the principle that we can see and detect the face of a person or multiple people in a single image frame and it works on the application of open CV.

Number Plate Detector

this project works on the principle that we can bring the picture of the number plates and then it will take the screenshots of similar number plates and store it in folder and it works on the application of open CV.

Document Scanner
